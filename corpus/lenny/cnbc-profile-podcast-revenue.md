# Lenny Rachitsky Profile: How His Podcast Brings in $500K+/Year — CNBC
**Source:** https://www.cnbc.com/2024/01/11/lenny-rachitsky-podcast-brings-in-more-than-500k-a-year-heres-how.html
**Published:** January 11, 2024
**Type:** Profile / business breakdown

## Background
- Started newsletter in 2019 about product management
- 574,000 subscribers at time of article (many paid) → over $500K/year from newsletter alone
- Ran Maven course twice in 2021 but stopped: "wasn't bringing me enough joy" — "sapping me of energy"
- Age: 42 at time of article

## Podcast Origin Story
- March 2022: Guest on 20VC with Harry Stebbings
- After recording, Stebbings said: "Lenny, you need to do a podcast"
- That "pushed me over the edge"
- Spent several months preparing: reading interviewing books, getting equipment, pre-recording ~10 episodes
- First guests: Julie Zhuo (ex-Facebook VP Design) and Shreyas Doshi (ex-Google/Twitter PM)
- First episode: June 2022

## Podcast Business Model
- Had existing ad demand from newsletter advertisers
- Started with 1 episode/week, moved to 2/week: "to be competitive, you want to do two a week"
- Each episode ~1 hour featuring interview with product/growth leader
- Focus: "very tactical, concrete advice" about building products
- Now brings in >$500K/year

## Current Operations
- Producer helps with audio and show notes
- Hired people for social media presence
- Spends ~4-5 hours/week on the podcast
- "It's amazing... with the newsletter I get to share my own insights, and with the podcast I get to learn from others. I can't imagine any better thing I could be doing for work."

## Daily Feedback
"Basically every day I get a message from someone about how impactful the podcast has been to them."
