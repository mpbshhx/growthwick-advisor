## What is Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)?

AEO is the practice of optimizing content to appear in AI-generated answers — from Google's AI Overviews, Perplexity, ChatGPT, and other AI assistants that synthesize answers from web content.

Traditional SEO: Rank in the 10 blue links
AEO: Get cited IN the AI-generated answer

## Why AEO Matters Now

- Google AI Overviews now appear for ~20%+ of queries (growing)
- Perplexity, ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini are being used for research
- Voice search (Siri, Alexa, Google Assistant) delivers single spoken answers
- Zero-click searches are increasing — users get answers without clicking

**The threat**: If AI answers the question, organic traffic decreases even with high rankings.
**The opportunity**: If your content is CITED as the source, you get brand authority + some traffic.

## Neil Patel's AEO Framework

### 1. Answer Questions Directly and Concisely
AI models favor content that clearly and directly answers the question:
- Put the direct answer in the first 1-2 sentences (inverted pyramid structure)
- Don't bury the answer in long-winded intros
- Use the question as a subheading, then answer it immediately below

### 2. Structure Content for AI Parsing
- Use clear headings (H2, H3) that match likely query phrasing
- Use bullet points and numbered lists for step-by-step content
- Use FAQ sections at the end of posts
- Include summary boxes or TL;DR sections

### 3. Establish Topical Authority
AI models cite sources they recognize as authoritative:
- Build a comprehensive content cluster around your core topics
- Get mentioned/cited by authoritative publications in your space
- Earn high-quality backlinks from recognized industry sources

### 4. Schema Markup
Structured data helps AI understand and extract your content:
- FAQPage schema — signals Q&A content
- HowTo schema — signals process/tutorial content
- Article schema — author credentials, publication date

### 5. Factual Accuracy and Sourcing
AI models are trained to prefer citable, accurate content:
- Cite original research and data
- Include statistics with sources
- Update content regularly to maintain accuracy

### 6. Entity Optimization
AI understands entities (people, places, brands, concepts), not just keywords:
- Mention your brand name consistently
- Build "entity authority" through mentions across the web
- Wikipedia page (if eligible) and Google Knowledge Panel boost entity recognition

## AEO vs. Traditional SEO

| | Traditional SEO | AEO |
|---|---|---|
| Goal | Rank in top 10 | Get cited in AI answer |
| Success metric | Position, click-through rate | Citation rate, brand mentions |
| Content format | Long-form comprehensive | Direct, structured, authoritative |
| Main channel | Google organic | Google AI, Perplexity, ChatGPT, voice |

## Neil's Take
"The rules of the game are changing. You used to optimize for the algorithm. Now you optimize for AI systems that are reading your content and deciding whether it's worth citing. The fundamentals — expertise, authority, trust — matter more than ever. But how you demonstrate them is evolving."
