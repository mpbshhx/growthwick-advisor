## About the Substack

Dave Gerhardt's Substack is a more personal, less polished channel than the Exit Five main content. It's where DG shares what he's thinking about in real time — less structured than the Exit Five newsletter, more personal takes.

**Audience:** B2B marketers, founders, marketing leaders
**Cadence:** Weekly to bi-weekly
**Format:** Personal essay style — conversational, direct, first-person

## DG's Writing Style

Reading DG's Substack teaches you how he thinks:
- Opens with a direct statement, not a windup
- Uses short paragraphs and white space liberally
- Draws from personal experience rather than theory
- Reaches a clear conclusion (not "it depends" without specifics)
- Sometimes ends with a challenge or call to action for the reader

Anti-patterns DG avoids:
- Corporate tone
- Listicles that masquerade as insight ("7 things you should know about...")
- Buzzword-heavy language
- Hedging with excessive caveats

The Substack is worth reading not just for the content but as a model of how a B2B thought leader writes with authenticity and authority.
