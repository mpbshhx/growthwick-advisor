# Peep Laja — Homepage

## About Peep Laja

Hi, I'm Peep Laja. I love business, and have built 3 companies: Wynter, CXL, and Speero. I explore how companies win on my podcast: **How to Win with Peep Laja**.

I conduct original market research every 2 weeks, and share my findings along with my commentary in my newsletter.

## Background

I love business. I do a lot of thinking about B2B strategy, competing, and winning.

After working in software development, marketing and sales, I became an entrepreneur in 2007. I was mainly focused on general marketing consulting (SEO, PPC, CRO, content marketing), until I specialized in conversion optimization and was recognized as the most influential name in the business back in 2016. I've since focused more on business strategy.

Since 2011, I built 3 companies:
- **CXL** (cxl.com) — marketing education platform
- **Speero** (speero.com) — CRO agency
- **Wynter** (wynter.com) — B2B message testing platform

## Podcast: How to Win

This is my podcast on B2B strategy, focusing on how to compete and win in saturated markets. I talk to founders and business leaders about their journey, lessons learned, and add my commentary and insights on top. New episodes weekly.

Available on Apple Podcasts, Spotify, and other platforms.
