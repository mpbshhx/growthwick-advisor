## Key Frameworks

### Agentic-Led Growth (ALG)

A new GTM model where AI agents handle key parts of the customer acquisition and expansion process. Three layers:
1. **Intelligence Layer** — AI that understands what customers need and where they are in their journey
2. **Activation Layer** — AI agents that take action (outreach, personalization, onboarding)
3. **Learning Layer** — Systems that get smarter from every interaction

This is the model HubSpot is rebuilding its GTM around.

### The AI Marketing Generalist

The marketer of the future is a generalist who:
- Can orchestrate AI tools across the full marketing stack
- Has deep strategic thinking (AI handles execution)
- Can design and manage hybrid human+AI workflows
- Operates at the intersection of product marketing, growth, and brand

### Marketing Team for the Agentic Age

Traditional marketing org: siloed specialists → hand-off chains → slow execution

Agentic-age org:
- Intelligence system at the center (AI + data)
- Small team of high-judgment generalists who direct the system
- AI agents handling content creation, distribution, personalization, and analysis
- Continuous feedback loops replacing quarterly planning
