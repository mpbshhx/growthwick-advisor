## Kieran Flanagan's AI Marketing Framework

Based on Kieran's public writing, talks, and Substack positioning:

### The 3 Levels of AI Marketing Adoption

**Level 1: AI as a Tool**
- Using AI for one-off tasks (write this email, summarize this brief)
- No systems built, no compounding value
- Most marketers stuck here

**Level 2: AI as a Workflow**
- Building repeatable AI-powered processes
- AI handles execution, human handles strategy and QC
- 3-5x productivity gains possible here

**Level 3: AI as a System**
- AI agents operating autonomously
- Marketing intelligence that compounds over time
- The "agentic marketing" future Kieran is building toward

### Practical AI Use Cases for Marketers (from Kieran's content)

**Content Production**
- AI drafts, humans edit and add POV
- Volume increases 5-10x without quality loss (with good prompts)

**SEO**
- AI for keyword clustering, content briefs, meta descriptions
- Human judgment for topical authority strategy

**Personalization at Scale**
- AI segments audiences and personalizes messaging
- Human sets the segmentation logic and brand voice

**Competitive Intelligence**
- AI monitors competitor content, pricing, positioning changes
- Human interprets strategic implications

**GTM Execution**
- AI handles routine GTM tasks (outreach drafts, follow-ups, reporting)
- Human focuses on relationships and strategy

### Kieran's Key Principles
1. **Start with the output, work backward to the prompt** — know what great looks like before generating
2. **Build AI habits, not one-off experiments** — consistency compounds
3. **AI amplifies your thinking, not replaces it** — the marketer's judgment is the moat
4. **Agentic-led growth is the next frontier** — autonomous AI running GTM loops
