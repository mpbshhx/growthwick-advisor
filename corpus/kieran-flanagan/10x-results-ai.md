## The 10X AI Results Framework (from Kieran's public content)

### What "10X Results" Actually Means
Kieran's definition: Not doing the same work 10x faster. Doing work that was previously impossible for one person — running campaigns, analyzing data, creating content, and optimizing simultaneously.

### The AI Second Brain (May 2026 post — 128 likes, most engaged post)
"I built an AI Second Brain. It's made me a 10x better GTM leader. The single most impactful thing I've built with AI, and why every GTM leader needs one."

**What an AI Second Brain contains:**
- Personal knowledge base fed into AI context
- Historical decisions and reasoning
- Competitor intelligence updated regularly
- Customer insights and research
- Strategic frameworks and playbooks

**How it works:**
1. Capture all inputs (calls, docs, research, competitor content) into structured format
2. Feed into AI with context about your role, company, market
3. Query it for synthesis, pattern recognition, decision support
4. AI becomes your external memory + analytical layer

### The 10X Leverage Points for GTM Leaders

**1. Speed of Strategy Development**
Traditional: Research → synthesize → workshop → align → document = weeks
AI-assisted: Research → AI synthesis → human review → document = hours

**2. Personalization at Scale**
Traditional: Personalized outreach to 10 accounts/week (human limit)
AI-assisted: Personalized outreach to 100+ accounts/week (AI handles drafts, human reviews)

**3. Content Production**
Traditional: 2-3 pieces/week per marketer
AI-assisted: 10-20 pieces/week with maintained quality (with proper workflow)

**4. Competitive Intelligence**
Traditional: Quarterly competitive reviews
AI-assisted: Weekly or daily intelligence updates with automatic alerts

**5. Agentic Execution**
Emerging: AI agents run entire GTM programs autonomously
Human role shifts to: strategy, relationships, brand judgment, ethical guardrails

### Kieran's Warning
"Most marketers are using AI as a fancy spell-checker. The ones getting 10X results are using it as a strategic thinking partner and autonomous executor."

### The Mindset Shift Required
- From: "AI does my tasks faster"
- To: "AI enables work that was previously impossible"

## Key Principles
1. **Systems > one-off prompts** — Build recurring AI workflows
2. **Context is everything** — AI is only as good as the context you give it
3. **Human judgment at the decision point** — AI proposes, human disposes
4. **Compound over time** — AI systems get smarter as they accumulate more of your context
