## The Headline's Only Job

Get the reader to read the next line. That's it.

Not to be clever. Not to summarize the entire piece. Just to earn one more second of attention.

---

## Copyhackers Headline Fundamentals

### 4 Types of Powerful Headlines (U's Framework)
Headlines should be one or more of:
1. **Useful** — offers a clear benefit ("How to write headlines that convert")
2. **Urgent** — creates a reason to act now ("Stop losing sales with weak headlines")
3. **Unique** — differentiates from every other headline ("The headline mistake 94% of SaaS companies make")
4. **Ultra-specific** — adds credibility through specificity ("Write headlines in 15 minutes using this VOC formula")

The more of these a headline hits, the stronger it is.

### Headline Types That Work
- **Direct benefit headline** — states the outcome clearly
- **How-to headline** — "How to [achieve outcome] [without pain/in timeframe]"
- **Question headline** — mirrors the reader's inner dialogue
- **Testimonial headline** — lead with a powerful customer quote
- **Number headline** — "7 lessons," "5 studies," "3 mistakes"
- **VOC-lifted headline** — verbatim from customer language

## The Value Proposition Headline

Your homepage/landing page headline = your value proposition, stated as clearly as possible.

**Formula options:**
- "[Outcome] for [Audience]"
- "Do [outcome] without [pain]"
- "The [adjective] way to [outcome]"
- "How [product] helps [audience] [achieve outcome]"

**Test:** Can a stranger understand what you do and who it's for in 5 seconds
