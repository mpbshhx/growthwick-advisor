# The Future of Business Is AI, or Obsolete

By Paul Roetzer

With each advancement in AI, it's becoming more apparent that there will be three types of businesses in every industry: **AI Native, AI Emergent and Obsolete**.

This applies across: retailers, ecommerce shops, marketing agencies, event businesses, media companies, law firms, medical practices, artists, writers, graphic designers, financial advisors, banks, insurance brokers, software makers, game and app developers, real estate brokers, consumer products makers, hotels, restaurants, manufacturers, distributors, educational institutions.

## Why Now? Why Is This Moment Different?

- Data continues to expand exponentially — and our need to understand and act on it
- Consumers demand convenience and personalization in both B2B and B2C
- Leadership expects ever-improving performance with greater efficiency
- Predicting human behavior and business outcomes is essential to reducing uncertainty
- Cloud computing power continues to rise; cost of building AI applications continues to fall
- Google, Microsoft, and Amazon rapidly expanding AI research and cloud offerings
- Venture capital pouring into AI-powered startups across every industry
- Rate of AI-powered innovation is accelerating, opening seemingly endless possibilities

**The result: Perfect storm for wide-scale disruption, and once-in-a-lifetime wealth creation and career advancement.**

## The Three Types of Businesses

### 1. AI Native

AI Native companies would not exist without the language, vision, and predictive applications AI enables. Built from the ground up with AI at the core.

Characteristics:
- More resource-efficient than established competitors
- Think bigger about what's possible
- Put a premium on data
- Build smarter businesses that will rise to the top of their industries

Investment thesis: Pick any industry, find a niche, build a smarter solution.

### 2. AI Emergent

AI Emergent companies are established organizations that move quickly to adopt and scale AI across all areas.

Characteristics:
- Visionary leaders who see rapid AI advancement and invest resources accordingly
- Expanding AI and ML talent pools
- Innovate faster than competition (potentially through venture studios or R&D labs)
- Excel at personalization across marketing, sales, and service
- Have data, customer bases and infrastructure to withstand AI Native companies — if they move fast enough

**Netflix example:** From DVD deliveries in the late 90s to dominant streaming company powered by AI in almost every facet of its business.

### 3. Obsolete

Obsolete companies wait for the business world to get smarter around them.

Behaviors:
- Don't seek knowledge about AI
- Leaders resist change
- Don't run pilot tests to prove AI's value
- Think of AI as just the next overhyped buzzword

By the time they act, AI Native and AI Emergent companies have seized control of the market.

## Agency Example: The Stark Contrast

**Agency A (AI Emergent):**
- Aggressively adopts AI across core services (analytics, copywriting, advertising creative, paid media management, email marketing, graphic design)
- Over one year: produces same quantity of work in 20% less time, quality increases
- Redistributes 20% time savings to invest in new marketing and sales campaigns
- Continually focuses team on more fulfilling work: strategy, creativity, client relationships
- Result: Happy employees, loyal clients, strong financial future

**Agency B (Obsolete):**
- Sticks to traditional model of 70-80% utilization rates
- So busy optimizing billable hours and cutting costs it doesn't prioritize AI
- Result: Employees who feel overworked and undervalued, clients wanting more value, leaders wondering where it all went wrong

## Key Insight

"AI doesn't replace the need to develop valued products and services, create amazing customer experiences, build a strong brand, and continually invest in your talent and culture.

But, the competitive advantages gained by AI Native and Emergent companies, that also solve for strong business foundations, will be too great to overcome in the coming years."

Being bold, charting a new path — this is the opportunity. The window is closing but still open. Choose to be an AI Native or AI Emergent company.
