# Three Steps to Start Integrating AI and AI Agents Into Your Marketing Workflows

**Marketing AI Institute | June 2026**

## The Data Behind the Demand

The 2026 State of AI for Business Report surveyed more than 2,100 business professionals — 86% B2B marketers — on what AI training they want most:

- **58%** want training on integrating AI into their existing workflows
- **51%** want to learn how to use AI agents in their work

The demand is clear. The "how" is harder.

## The Three-Step Framework (Rachel Woods, Founder & CEO, The AI Momentum Protocols)

### Step 1: Own the playbook, rent the tech.

Before touching any tool, the best teams think through the playbook: What they want the AI to do and how.

- Design from business problems and processes, not from tool capabilities
- The playbook is the asset you own — the tool is a rental
- Tools change. A well-designed playbook holds.

### Step 2: Start with an expert in the loop, then earn your way out.

Don't start by automating. Instead:

1. Build the simplest version with AI handling what it can
2. Have a human reviewing everything
3. Every correction goes back into the instructions
4. Earn automation by progressively removing yourself from the loop
5. Trust must be built before autonomy is granted

### Step 3: Prioritize momentum over perfection.

- Find the smallest useful playbook you can build, get it working
- Think of playbooks like Lego blocks: once the first one runs, snap the next on
- Small wins compound
- Teams that wait for the perfect system never build anything

## Key Principle

The approach is not to get caught up in the tools or expect them to do all the heavy lifting. Focus on the process and the solution you seek. The tools, with human oversight, will help you get there.
