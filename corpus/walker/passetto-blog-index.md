## About Passetto

Passetto is a GTM analytics technology and strategic advisory firm. Their technology combines CRM & Finance data into a department-agnostic data model, empowering C-Level executives with new views, KPIs, and insights related to investment performance across the customer lifecycle.

## Core Services

### GTM Health Checks & Performance Monitoring
Recurring standardized assessments of entire GTM performance. Identifies opportunities to increase efficiency, productivity, and profitability. Proprietary analytics find early warning signs and risks.

### Value Creation Roadmap via GTM Optimization
Helps build, plan, manage and execute against a long-term, integrated GTM Strategic roadmap at the intersection of RevOps, Finance, Sales, and Marketing. Department-agnostic data and analytics help prioritize and sequence initiatives based on data and impact.

### GTM First Team Strategic Advisory & Consulting
Third-party expertise helping B2B SaaS companies increase executive alignment, accelerate decision making, improve decision confidence, avoid risks, and communicate a compelling data-backed narrative to the board.

## Key Value Propositions

- **Combine Financial Data & CRM Data** — Purpose-built technology connects financial and GTM data for a unified view, calculating standardized KPIs and measuring unit economics
- **Turn Financial KPIs into GTM Strategic Priorities** — Help Finance & GTM Leaders analyze performance, uncover opportunities, model impact, and validate decisions
- **Increase Sales Development Productivity** — Find low productivity activities and re-focus them to best opportunities
- **Optimize Marketing Strategy and Investments** — Signal-based Analytics for marketing strategy, investment allocations, and conversion journeys
- **Optimize Pipeline Quality, Sales Velocity, and Forecasting Accuracy** — Connect Signals to Revenue for higher quality opportunities
