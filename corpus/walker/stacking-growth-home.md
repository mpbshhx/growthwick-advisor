## About Stacking Growth
Stacking Growth is Chris Walker's B2B marketing podcast and content hub focused on:
- Demand generation strategy
- Dark funnel marketing
- B2B pipeline building
- Revenue attribution
- GTM strategy for B2B SaaS

## About Chris Walker
- Founder & Former CEO of Refine Labs (now Chairman)
- Host of Stacking Growth podcast
- Pioneer of the "dark funnel" concept in B2B marketing
- Advocate for demand generation over lead generation
- Known for: challenging traditional B2B marketing attribution models

## Core Themes (from Refine Labs corpus)
- The dark funnel: buyers research independently before engaging with sales
- Brand, Demand, Expand framework for B2B marketing structure
- Why lead gen metrics fail to capture true pipeline health
- The attribution mirage: why most B2B attribution is wrong
- Demand philosophy: create demand, don't just capture it
