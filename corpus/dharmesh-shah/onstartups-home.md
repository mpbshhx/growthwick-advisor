## Key Themes in Dharmesh's Work

1. **Inbound over outbound** — pull people toward you with value, don't interrupt them
2. **Culture as competitive moat** — the HubSpot Culture Code has 5M+ views and shaped how a generation of SaaS companies think about values
3. **SaaS-specific founder advice** — product-market fit, fundraising, freemium strategy, hiring
4. **AI as the next frontier** — actively building and investing in AI (agent.ai, WordPlay.com)
5. **Simple frameworks** — FlashTags, HEART culture model, freemium alternatives
