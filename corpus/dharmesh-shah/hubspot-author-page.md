# Dharmesh Shah — HubSpot Marketing Blog Author Page

## Bio

Dharmesh Shah is the **Founder and CTO of HubSpot**, where he has spent nearly two decades building one of the most influential platforms in modern marketing and sales.

- **Role:** Founder & CTO, HubSpot
- **Co-authored:** *Inbound Marketing* — foundational book that defined the inbound methodology
- **Personal platforms:** OnStartups.com, Connecting Dots newsletter
- **Education:** Master's degree from MIT
- **Focus areas:** Product, code, company culture, AI, startups, scale-ups

## Key Contributions

As the architect behind HubSpot's product vision, Dharmesh helped shape how millions of businesses attract, engage, and delight customers.

He remains a driving force in HubSpot's mission: "Help companies grow better."
