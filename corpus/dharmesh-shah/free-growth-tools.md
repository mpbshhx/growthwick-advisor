# The Free Growth Tools I Recommend For Modern Businesses — Dharmesh Shah

## Core Insight

Most growth problems come not from ideas, but from **execution**. You might have a growth-driven team, great vision, and dedication — but that's not enough.

You need a powerful set of tools that leverage every stage of the customer experience: first point of contact → sales/marketing process → lifetime of the customer.

## The Growth Stack Foundation

Every growth stack needs three pillars:
1. **CRM** — foundation for all other tools; stores all customer data
2. **Marketing platform** — attract the right people, convert leads, communicate through purchase and beyond
3. **Sales platform** — identify the right people, connect, close

## Free Tools by Category

### CRM
**1. HubSpot CRM**
- Foundation of digital systems — tracks every sales interaction, stores marketing leads and customer data
- Traditional CRMs are hard to implement; HubSpot's is clean and intuitive
- Hard truth: "It's impossible to grow and scale your business without a CRM"

### Marketing Tools
**2. Trello**
- Best for visualizing progress on ideas, projects, experiments simultaneously
- Real example: Kieran Flanagan (HubSpot VP Marketing) used it to track all growth experiments from start to finish — team spread across US and Europe, all organized
- Each idea/project/experiment lives on its own card with notes, tasks, due dates

**3. Hotjar Heat Maps & Visitor Recordings**
- Data beats intuition
- Heat maps and Visitor Recordings: show clicks, taps, scrolling behavior
- Reveals what people care about on your site
- Helps find biggest improvement and testing opportunities
- "You can't really know what your audience wants without getting inside the heads of people who visit your site"

**4. Google Keyword Planner**
- SEO is key to traffic growth
- Narrows down right keywords, gauges competition, shows search volume
- Good starting point before more advanced SEO tools

**5. GrowthBot** (Dharmesh's own creation)
- Chatbot for growth professionals
- Connects to HubSpot, Google Analytics, and more
- Can answer: "How was organic traffic last month?" → instant answer
- Can also answer: "Which public SaaS companies are using HubSpot?" — data you didn't know you had

**6. HubSpot Lead Flows**
- Common mistake: spending all time driving traffic, not converting it to leads
- Lead Flows: pop-ups, dropdown banners, slide-ins added to website pages
- Directs interested readers to in-depth content behind lead-generating forms
- No technical setup or coding required — can set up in minutes

**7. Buffer**
- Social media scheduling across Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, Instagram, Google+
- "Social media is an amplifier. If you build a following, it's a great way to spread your content further and wider."
- Text, links, photos, videos — schedule immediately or for later

**8. Canva**
- Makes visual content creation fast for non-designers
- Better visuals = more engagement = more traffic = business growth
- Important for businesses with limited design resources

### Sales Tools
**9. join.me**
- Conference calls and screen sharing
- Fast, reliable, intuitive for sales team demos
- Integrates with HubSpot CRM to start meetings directly from contact timeline

**10. HubSpot Sales Email Templates**
- Sales reps write the same emails repeatedly — wasteful
- Templates = personalized, pre-built emails accessible from inbox
- Shared library = everyone uses high-performing templates
- Data on open/click rates → hone in on best approaches

**11. HubSpot Email Open Tracking**
- Real-time desktop notifications when emails are opened
- Shows: who opened, when, on which device, where
- Full notification stream per email

## The Long-Term Goal

These tools are a starting point for companies growing from startup to sustainable. Down the road: integrate them into a true growth stack.

Growth stack = all tools connected and talking to each other across CRM, marketing, and sales.
