## Core Methodology Summary

The Obviously Awesome process helps companies:
1. Identify what makes their product genuinely different
2. Find the customers who care most about that difference
3. Choose the right market context to make value obvious
4. Align go-to-market around the positioning

The process is grounded in April's experience repositioning 7 companies to successful exits and advising hundreds of scaling B2B tech companies.
