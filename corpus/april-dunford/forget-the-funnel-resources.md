# Forget the Funnel — Customer-Led Growth Resources

## Overview

Forget the Funnel is a resource hub for SaaS marketing and growth, focusing on the **Customer-Led Growth™** framework.

## Content Available

The site offers:
- Podcast episodes
- Workshops
- Expert interviews
- Q&A sessions
- Templates and tools

## Core Framework: Customer-Led Growth™

Customer-Led Growth positions customers at the center of SaaS marketing strategy — instead of funnel-based acquisition, it focuses on deeply understanding what makes existing customers successful and designing growth around that.

## Relevance to Positioning

Forget the Funnel's approach aligns closely with April Dunford's positioning methodology:
- Start with customers who have already found success with the product
- Understand what value they actually received
- Use that insight to sharpen positioning and messaging for acquisition
